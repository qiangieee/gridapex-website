Who We Are

Your AI-Ready Real Estate Partner

Grid Apex is a startup building the next generation of AI-ready data centers in Japan. Our first 100MW campus is purpose-engineered from day one for the extreme power density, advanced cooling, and rapid scalability that AI workloads demand.

Our Technology

Why Grid Apex Is AI-First

Every design decision — from the foundation slab to the cooling manifolds — is made with the unique demands of AI compute in mind.

01

Liquid Cooling by Design

Our warm shells arrive with liquid cooling infrastructure pre-installed — chilled water distribution, manifold connections, and rear-door heat exchanger capacity — eliminating the #1 bottleneck for high-density GPU deployment.

02

Prefabricated, Fast-Deploy Modules

Factory-built AI compute containers are manufactured in parallel with site work, pre-tested, and shipped ready to connect — compressing typical 18-month builds to 6 months or less.

03

Green Energy & Battery Storage

On-site solar, grid-scale battery systems, and Japan's renewable energy credits ensure that AI workloads run on clean power — meeting ESG requirements and reducing long-term operating costs.

04

100MW Power-Dense Foundation

Redundant utility feeds, on-site backup generation, and an electrical distribution system rated for 100+ kW per rack — ensuring the campus can absorb the densest GPU hardware available today and tomorrow.
